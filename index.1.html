THE BAND

That's what Call Me Joe delivers. Some are old. Some are new. All are timeless songs for a great Saturday night out on the town. Songs that stick like honey and make you smile over your drink. And songs that make you want to sing along, move along, and get the evening started. Call Me Joe plays well-known rock songs—from Springsteen to Kravitz, from Stones to Police—with some added flavour of their own. Check out some sound clips to get an impression.
